What We Believe

Mission

Crossings Kids exists to help kids find and follow Jesus.

Vision

We are a community of kids who want to live by faith, be a voice of hope, and be known by love in our schools and communities.

Purpose

Partner with parents in building a foundation of faith in Jesus. Crossings Kids partners with parents to:
  • Develop children as Christ-followers and teach them to share their faith.
  • Connect children to God through worship.
  • Lead children to communicate with God through prayer.
  • Grow children in knowledge and wisdom.
  • Assist children in forming relationships with one another and adult volunteers.
