Community Center Driver Transportation Covenant

Leaders and volunteers are expected to aspire to live a life committed to follow Jesus, and aspire to live by faith, be a voice of hope and known by love. Ministry leaders and volunteers agree to adhere to these standards, and agree that when these standards are violated, they submit to correction and, if warranted, removal from leadership or service in that ministry.

  1. I understand that in my serving role I am doing work for the Kingdom of God. I agree to conduct myself in a manner that will be consistent with the values and mission of Crossings both in my volunteer role and in my daily life. (1 John 2:5-6)
  2. I understand that as I volunteer, I am expected to treat others as Jesus would treat them if He were here in my place. This includes: thinking the best of one another and going through the proper channels in conflict. (see Matthew 18)
  3. I agree as a volunteer I agree to ensure not exceed the maximum capacity of the vehicle, drive safely, drive conservatively, not to drive beyond approved guidelines, and uphold our policy of driver will not be alone with any participants or another volunteer. There needs to be a minimum of 3 people in the vehicle.
  4. I understand that as a volunteer, I am here to assist in carrying out the mission of the transportation ministry. I understand that considerable responsibility comes with providing a safe environment while driving participants involved in this ministry.
